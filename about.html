About SmokeHouse Central

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate pitmasters, SmokeHouse Central has grown from a small online forum into a thriving community of BBQ enthusiasts. Our mission is to preserve and share the time-honored traditions of meat smoking while embracing modern techniques and innovations.

Quick Facts

  • 50,000+ active community members
  • 1,000+ verified recipes
  • 200+ expert contributors
  • Weekly live smoking sessions

Our Mission
